This is a collection of models I have used for the Let's Do Elections mock government server over the past two years. These models are somewhat simplified, as I can't simulate events and spending in an easy way on the browser.

NEW Model 3.0 Beta

An experimental model that aims to tackle the unpredictability of using the 2.0 Model. Includes a new Progressive/Conservative axis for candidates. This model may be unreliable, as it is brand new and still in beta testing.

Use Model 3.0 (BETA)


Released July 2023

Model 2.0

The Let's Do Elections 2 model repurposed for general use. Used in a wide variety of mock governments, this is our most popular model.

Released December 2022

Legacy Model (1.0)
